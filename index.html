BB FIRAC

Structured Case Analysis Rooted in Legal Clarity

The BB FIRAC Method is a refined case summarisation framework tailored for South African constitutional jurisprudence. Each case note distils the judgment into five core components.

This structured format ensures uniformity, analytical depth, and paragraph-level traceability across every case.

  • Facts - The background and procedural posture of the case
  • Issues - The constitutional or legal questions under review
  • Rules - The statutory, constitutional, or judicial authorities applied
  • Application - The Court's reasoning in applying the law to the facts
  • Conclusion - The final decision and its legal implications
